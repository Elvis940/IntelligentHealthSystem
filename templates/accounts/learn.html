Revolutionizing Healthcare in Rwanda

My dissertation project transforming patient care at Ruhengeri Level II Teaching Hospital

The Healthcare Challenge

Key problems identified in my dissertation research at Ruhengeri Hospital

  • Manual Data Management

    Patient records handled through paper-based systems and basic Excel files, leading to inefficiencies and errors.

  • Limited Decision Support

    No systems for personalized treatment recommendations based on patient history and demographics.

  • Poor Data Visualization

    Lack of real-time dashboards for monitoring disease trends and hospital performance.

Research Findings

My surveys with 50 hospital staff revealed:

  • 82% reported difficulty accessing patient histories
  • 76% wanted better visualization tools
  • 68% supported personalized treatment systems

Our Intelligent Solution

How this system addresses Rwanda's healthcare technology gaps

Personalized Medicine

Rule-based engine providing treatment suggestions tailored to patient demographics and Rwandan healthcare guidelines.

Data Visualization

Interactive dashboards showing disease prevalence, treatment outcomes, and hospital resource allocation.

Digital Records

Secure patient data management system with role-based access control and audit trails.

Research Methodology

Agile development approach as detailed in Chapter 3 of my dissertation

Requirement Gathering

Conducted surveys and interviews with 50 staff members at Ruhengeri Hospital to identify pain points and system requirements.

Prototype Development

Built initial prototypes using Agile Scrum methodology with 2-week sprints for iterative feedback.

User Testing

Conducted three testing cycles with hospital staff to refine interfaces and functionality.

Final Deployment

Implemented the system using Docker containers for easy deployment in hospital environments.
